# Roadmap: Solum Real Estate Group Website

## Overview

Build a professional website that establishes Solum as a credible, capable real estate investment company. Start with infrastructure and core credibility messaging (team narrative and investment philosophy), then add engagement mechanisms to convert lenders, brokers, and property owners into active relationships.

## Domain Expertise

None

## Phases

- [ ] **Phase 1: Foundation & Infrastructure** - Set up site structure, design system, and GitHub Pages deployment
- [ ] **Phase 2: Company Story** - Build team bios and partnership narrative sections
- [ ] **Phase 3: Investment Philosophy & Market Focus** - Create pages articulating investment approach and target markets
- [ ] **Phase 4: Contact & Engagement** - Add contact form, CTAs, and optimize for inbound conversion
